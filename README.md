# MonitoringWPF
A monitoring application made with WPF.

You can have a look on your's OS, its architecture, the GPU and the last version installed.
I drew a custom design to show how much the cpu is used.
You can have a look on your RAM (as a whole, the used and free parts.)
You can check all your ready drives.
And finally the network informations;
Every kilobytes sent (upload) and received (download) plus your download speed in red !

It's a prototype, for the motherboard's global temp don't work for the moment.
Thanks for reading !
